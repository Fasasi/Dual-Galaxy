ABOUT

The 3rd Annual Beantown Autumn Festival!

The Beantown Autumn Festival, hosted by massALLY, is a community event open to all of our neighbors in the surrounding Boston communities. Kids under 13 play and eat for free! We hope you join us and bring your family & friends for fun, food and friendship. In early November, the 4th Annual Boston Gridiron Classic (BGC) will take place and serves to raise funds for the Beantown Autumn Festival. We invite you to come watch some friendly competition, as well!
